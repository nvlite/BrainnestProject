28. Typhoon The term "typhoon" means a huge, rotating tropical storm. The name for this storm comes directly from Typhon. According to Greek mythology, he was the storm giant and the father of all monsters. 29. Zephyr A soft and gentle west wind is called a "zephyr." This weather pattern also gets its name from Greek mythology. Zephyrus was the Greek god of the west wind.
